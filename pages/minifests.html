Minifests

Short for Mini Festival. Every month, 70/80P makes learning materials for 3 films which are curated by theme. Every module comes with information on the film’s director, production, reception, and much more. Order a printed copy here (trust us, it feels so much better to hold it in your hands!) PDFs will be uploaded after each MiniFest run.

Click on the film to view its online module.

March

Women's Month

We’re glad to say that 7080P started off with films that all feature strong female narratives. They all star wonderful leading ladies (Aunor, Koronel, Tolentino, Alajar, Andolong) whose determined characters won’t take no for an answer. Sometimes they help each other too. (Yes, these all pass the Bechdel test!)

The MDL Review

Mike De Leon’s made his comeback with this year’s Citizen Jake, but have you seen the films that helped him shape his artistic vision? We’ve got a 1970s Baguio romance and a disco-drugs extravaganza - the changing genres and subject matter just go to show how flexible MDL really is.
