Mike de Leon

1980 | Comedy-Musical | 1h 44m

kakabakaba ka ba?

Disco, drug wars, and a whole lot of dancing.

A fun, music-loving barkada somehow gets caught up in a dastardly drug war between the Japanese and the Chinese. Chaos (and some musical hijinx) ensue. Mike de Leon's colorful comedy gives clever commentary on the unfortunate place that the Philippines seems to always find itself in: at the mercy of the wants and needs of foreigners.
