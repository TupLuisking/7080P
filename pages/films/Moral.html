Moral

Somewhere in this film, you will find yourself.

A superior achievement for focusing on four Filipinas and being directed by a Filipina director, Moral follows friends Kathy, Joey, Sylvia, and Maritess as they search for the meaning of life, femininity, and their roles in modern Filipino society. Although each woman has a different goal in life, they discover life's many complications - but at least they have each other.
