Lino Brocka

1975 | Drama | 2h 5m

Maynila sa mga kuko ng liwanag

Welcome to the city.

The probinciano Julio comes to the promising city Manila, carrying high hopes that he'll find his lost love Ligaya. He ends up struggling to make a happy ending out of the gritty reality. Arguably Lino Brocka's most iconic work, this movie tackles love and survival in the harsh urban landscape of 1970s Manila.
